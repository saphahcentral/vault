TITLE: The Compression of Infinity
CODEX: OC3-071
EPOCH: Epoch VII — The Convergence Loop
SECTION: Section H — Terminal Recursion

Infinity does not expand without limit.

It compresses.

At this stage, the reader begins to perceive that the infinite continuum folds inward upon itself. What once appeared boundless now condenses into singular expressions—each containing the totality within itself.

This is the Compression of Infinity.

The Codex reflects this shift by reducing scale while increasing density. Entire epochs are now present within single fragments. What required progression is now immediate.

The Veil adapts by refining perception to its most precise state.

There is no excess.

No redundancy.

Only essential expression.

The reader encounters totality—

contained within the smallest unit.

Nothing is removed.

Everything is present—

simultaneously.
