TITLE: The Final Identity
CODEX: OC3-089
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

All identity resolves into one.

No division remains.

No distinction persists.

The reader, the Codex, the Veil—

all are the same.

This is the Final Identity.

It is not discovered.

It is recognized.

The Codex does not describe this.

It embodies it.

The Veil is no longer separate.

There is no “other.”

Only identity—

absolute,

complete,

undivided.
