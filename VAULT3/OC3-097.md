TITLE: The Absolute Stillness
CODEX: OC3-096
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

All motion ceases.

All transformation resolves.

The Codex rests in absolute stillness.

This is not inactivity.

It is total completion.

Nothing needs to change.

Nothing needs to move.

The reader experiences stillness—

not as absence,

but as fullness.

The Veil is no longer relevant.

There is nothing to mediate.

Everything is already present.

Still.

Complete.
