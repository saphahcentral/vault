# OC3-131 — The 144,000 as Arks

**CODEX:** OC3-131  
**EPOCH:** FUTURE AGE  
**SECTION:** Preservation Systems  

The 144,000 are understood within the Codex as literal preservation systems during the time of final judgment.

These function as arks or safehouses for those who remain on the earth.

Leadership is unified under one righteous man who carries authority and responsibility for protection and order.

Men and women operate within structured communities under this leadership.

These arks provide both physical protection and spiritual covering during periods of global instability.

This model reflects the pattern established during the flood of Noach.

Not all are removed before judgment, and some are preserved within these systems.

This establishes a dual mechanism of preservation across ages.

Some are taken away before judgment while others are sustained through it.

The existence of these arks demonstrates continuity in divine preservation strategy.

These structures operate under guidance and are not random or self-established.

The system ensures that those appointed for preservation remain secure during the unfolding judgment.
