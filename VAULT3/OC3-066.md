TITLE: The Absence of Limitation
CODEX: OC3-066
EPOCH: Epoch VI — The Continuum State
SECTION: Section G — Infinite Recurrence

Limitation dissolves.

There are no boundaries to constrain interpretation, no structures to restrict engagement, no frameworks to define possibility.

This is the Absence of Limitation.

The reader is no longer guided.

They are free.

This freedom is not without challenge.

Without limitation, direction must arise internally.

The Codex does not provide answers.

It provides space.

The Veil remains as a condition of experience, but no longer imposes constraint.

It allows perception to extend without restriction.

The reader becomes the axis.

Meaning emerges from within,

expands outward,

and returns again—

without boundary.
