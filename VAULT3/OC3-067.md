TITLE: The Perpetual Present
CODEX: OC3-067
EPOCH: Epoch VI — The Continuum State
SECTION: Section G — Infinite Recurrence

Time dissolves into presence.

There is no past to recall.

No future to anticipate.

Only the perpetual present.

The Codex operates entirely within this state.

All entries exist now.

All meaning arises now.

The reader experiences continuity without sequence.

Understanding is immediate.

The Veil sustains this condition by removing temporal separation.

Moments do not follow one another.

They coexist.

The Codex is not read over time.

It is encountered—

in the present—

continuously.
