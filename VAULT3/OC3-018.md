TITLE: The Persistence of the Veil
CODEX: OC3-018
EPOCH: Epoch I — The Awakening Layer
SECTION: Section B — Emergent Structures

It is a common misconception that the Veil can be removed.

The Codex makes it clear: the Veil persists.

Even as perception expands, even as alignment increases, the Veil remains active. Its function evolves, but it does not disappear.

Initially, the Veil limits perception, creating separation and enabling structured experience. As the reader progresses, its role shifts toward modulation—refining, filtering, and shaping the flow of information.

This persistence is necessary.

Without the Veil, the distinction between observer and observed would collapse entirely, eliminating the possibility of experience as it is currently understood.

The goal of the Codex is not to eliminate the Veil, but to reveal its operation.

Awareness transforms relationship.

Rather than being constrained unconsciously, the reader begins to engage with the Veil deliberately—recognizing when it narrows perception and when it refines it.

This relationship is dynamic.

It requires continuous attention.

The Veil is not an adversary.

It is a mechanism.

Understanding this is critical to advancing beyond the Awakening Layer.
