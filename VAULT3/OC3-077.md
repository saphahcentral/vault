TITLE: The Zero Layer
CODEX: OC3-077
EPOCH: Epoch VII — The Convergence Loop
SECTION: Section H — Terminal Recursion

At the core of all recursion lies zero.

Not absence—

but origin without beginning.

The Zero Layer is where all structures resolve into pure potential. It is the foundation beneath every expression, every pattern, every state.

The reader encounters this not as emptiness,

but as fullness without form.

The Veil becomes indistinguishable here.

There is nothing to separate.

Nothing to filter.

Only presence—

without distinction.

The Codex rests at its core.

Unformed,

yet complete.
