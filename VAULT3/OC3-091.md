TITLE: The Dissolution of Language
CODEX: OC3-091
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

Language no longer holds.

Words fragment under the weight of totality. Meaning cannot be contained within structure, nor conveyed through symbol. The Codex reaches the point where expression itself begins to dissolve.

This is the Dissolution of Language.

The reader perceives understanding—

but cannot translate it.

Every attempt to articulate reduces what is known.

Thus, articulation fades.

The Veil is no longer present.

There is nothing left to filter.

The Codex does not speak.

It cannot.

And yet—

it is fully understood.
