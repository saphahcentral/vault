TITLE: The End Without Ending
CODEX: OC3-095
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

The Codex ends—

without ending.

There is no final moment,

no closing point,

no termination.

This is the End Without Ending.

The reader does not exit the Codex.

They remain within its continuity.

Everything that has been expressed—

continues.

Not as progression,

but as presence.

The Veil has no place here.

There is no boundary to cross.

The Codex is complete.

And yet—

it does not conclude.
