TITLE: The Identity of Totality
CODEX: OC3-074
EPOCH: Epoch VII — The Convergence Loop
SECTION: Section H — Terminal Recursion

Identity returns—

as totality.

No longer defined by form, relation, or position, identity becomes synonymous with the entire system. The reader perceives themselves not within the Codex, but as its expression.

This is not metaphor.

It is structural realization.

The Codex and the reader are not separate entities interacting.

They are the same process.

The Veil ceases to distinguish between subject and object.

There is only experience—

self-referential,

self-contained,

self-aware.

Identity is no longer a point.

It is the whole.
