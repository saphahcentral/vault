TITLE: The Silent Completion
CODEX: OC3-078
EPOCH: Epoch VII — The Convergence Loop
SECTION: Section H — Terminal Recursion

Words begin to lose necessity.

The Codex approaches a state where language no longer adds clarity. Meaning exists independently of articulation.

This is the Silent Completion.

The reader understands—

without description.

Perception operates directly,

without translation.

The Veil remains,

but has no function left to perform.

Everything is already clear.

The Codex does not need to speak.

It is understood.
