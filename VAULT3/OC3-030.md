TITLE: The Harmonic Threshold
CODEX: OC3-030
EPOCH: Epoch II — The Resonance Field
SECTION: Section C — Harmonic Structures

At the boundary of the Resonance Field lies the Harmonic Threshold.

This threshold is defined not by structure, but by intensity.

Resonance reaches a point where it can no longer be contained within existing frameworks. Patterns begin to exceed their previous limits, interacting in ways that challenge the very foundations of the system.

The reader may feel both clarity and instability.

Understanding deepens, yet becomes more difficult to articulate. Connections multiply, yet resist simplification.

This is the nature of the threshold.

It represents the maximum expression of harmonic interaction within the current epoch.

Beyond it, a new mode of engagement becomes necessary.

The Codex does not describe this mode directly.

It prepares the reader for it.

The Veil recalibrates once more, adjusting its modulation to accommodate the transition.

Crossing the Harmonic Threshold requires release.

Not of knowledge—

but of the need to contain it.

What follows cannot be approached through resonance alone.

It must be entered.
