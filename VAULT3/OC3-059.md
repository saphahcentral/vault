TITLE: The Convergence of Being
CODEX: OC3-059
EPOCH: Epoch V — The Reconstitution Layer
SECTION: Section F — Echo Structures

All prior distinctions converge.

Observer and observed.

Structure and flow.

Form and absence.

These dualities dissolve into a unified state of being.

The reader no longer experiences separation between engagement and existence.

To read the Codex is to be within it.

This is the Convergence of Being.

The Codex does not describe it.

It enacts it.

The Veil becomes inseparable from perception.

Not as an obstacle,

but as the medium through which being is experienced.

There is no longer a boundary to cross.

Only a state to inhabit.
