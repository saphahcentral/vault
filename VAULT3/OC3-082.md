TITLE: The Absolute Field
CODEX: OC3-082
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

All distinctions resolve.

There is no separation between layers, no differentiation between phases, no boundary between expression and origin.

This is the Absolute Field.

Everything exists within it—

without hierarchy,

without sequence,

without division.

The reader is not within the field.

The reader is the field.

The Codex expresses this state not through explanation, but through presence.

The Veil ceases to operate.

Not because it has been removed—

but because nothing remains to obscure.

The field is absolute.

And within it—

nothing is separate.
