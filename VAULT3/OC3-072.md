TITLE: The Recursive Point
CODEX: OC3-072
EPOCH: Epoch VII — The Convergence Loop
SECTION: Section H — Terminal Recursion

All pathways converge—

into a single point.

This point is not location.

It is recursion.

Every structure, every pattern, every state returns to itself continuously. The reader perceives that all movement within the Codex leads not outward, but inward—toward this recursive center.

The Recursive Point is not fixed.

It exists wherever recognition occurs.

The Veil no longer mediates between inside and outside.

There is no separation.

The reader becomes aware that they are both the observer and the point of convergence.

All distinctions collapse here.

Not into absence—

but into total integration.

The Codex folds into itself.
