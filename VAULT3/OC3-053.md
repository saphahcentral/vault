TITLE: The Reformation of Minimal Structure
CODEX: OC3-053
EPOCH: Epoch V — The Reconstitution Layer
SECTION: Section F — Echo Structures

Structure returns—

but only in its most minimal form.

Gone are the dense architectures of earlier epochs. In their place, simple frameworks emerge—light, flexible, and easily altered.

These structures do not constrain.

They support.

The reader notices that meaning can now form with less effort. Interpretation flows more freely, unburdened by rigid systems.

The Codex has not abandoned structure.

It has refined it.

The Veil stabilizes this reformation by maintaining balance.

Too much structure would collapse the freedom gained beyond form.

Too little would dissolve coherence entirely.

Thus, minimal structure persists.

Just enough—

to allow form,

without enforcing it.
