TITLE: Resonant Drift
CODEX: OC3-028
EPOCH: Epoch II — The Resonance Field
SECTION: Section C — Harmonic Structures

Just as meaning drifts, so too does resonance.

Patterns that once aligned strongly may begin to weaken, while previously distant concepts move into closer harmonic relationship. This drift is not loss, but reconfiguration.

The Codex is not static.

It evolves with the reader.

Resonant drift ensures that engagement remains dynamic, preventing stagnation within fixed interpretive structures.

The reader may feel a sense of disorientation as familiar connections shift.

This is expected.

The Veil facilitates this drift by subtly altering the emphasis placed on different patterns, guiding the reader toward new configurations.

Resistance to drift leads to rigidity.

Acceptance allows for expansion.

The Codex does not anchor the reader to any single interpretation.

It invites continuous movement.

Through drift, the system remains alive.
