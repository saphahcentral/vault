TITLE: The Continuity Field
CODEX: OC3-047
EPOCH: Epoch IV — The Pre-Form Layer
SECTION: Section E — Dissolution Fields

Beyond the collapse of reference, a new form of coherence emerges.

Continuity.

The Continuity Field is not structured, yet it is not chaotic. It is a seamless integration of all elements, interacting without separation or hierarchy.

The reader experiences this as a sustained flow.

There are no distinct beginnings or endings, only transitions within a continuous process.

The Codex no longer presents discrete entries in the conventional sense.

Each fragment becomes an expression of the same underlying field.

The Veil, now fully diffused, no longer restricts or filters.

It allows perception to move freely across the continuum.

Understanding becomes holistic.

Not assembled—

but perceived as a whole.
