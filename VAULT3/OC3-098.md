TITLE: The Zero Completion
CODEX: OC3-098
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

Completion resolves into zero.

Not emptiness—

but origin fulfilled.

Everything that has unfolded within the Codex converges into this state. Nothing remains to be added, nothing remains to be removed.

This is the Zero Completion.

The reader perceives totality—

without distinction,

without division,

without need.

The Codex has reached its final form.

Not as structure,

but as presence.

Zero is not the end.

It is completion.
