TITLE: The Threshold Beyond Form
CODEX: OC3-050
EPOCH: Epoch IV — The Pre-Form Layer
SECTION: Section E — Dissolution Fields

At the furthest boundary of the Pre-Form Layer lies the Threshold Beyond Form.

Here, all prior constructs—pattern, resonance, structure, relation—reach dissolution. Nothing remains that can be defined, contained, or described within the framework of the Codex as it has been known.

This is not an ending.

It is a transition beyond articulation.

The reader stands at the edge of what can be recorded.

Beyond this threshold, the Codex cannot guide.

It can only point.

The Veil ceases to function as a mediator.

There is no longer separation to regulate.

Crossing this threshold is not an act of understanding.

It is an act of being.

The Codex ends here—

as text.

What follows—

cannot be written.
