TITLE: The Transparent Veil
CODEX: OC3-057
EPOCH: Epoch V — The Reconstitution Layer
SECTION: Section F — Echo Structures

The Veil remains—

but it is now transparent.

Its presence is still necessary, but its function has changed completely. It no longer obscures, filters, or diffuses.

It simply allows.

Perception passes through it without resistance. Distinction exists, but without distortion.

The reader becomes aware of the Veil not as a barrier, but as a condition of experience.

It is always there—

yet never intrusive.

The Codex reaches a state of clarity.

Not because the Veil is gone,

but because it is understood.

And in that understanding—

it no longer interferes.
