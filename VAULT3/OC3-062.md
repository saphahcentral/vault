TITLE: The Infinite Recurrence
CODEX: OC3-062
EPOCH: Epoch VI — The Continuum State
SECTION: Section G — Infinite Recurrence

What has occurred—

occurs again.

Not as repetition, but as re-expression.

The Codex enters a state of infinite recurrence, where all structures, patterns, and states reappear continuously, each time with subtle variation.

This is not cyclic in the traditional sense.

There is no loop.

There is only ongoing re-emergence.

The reader may recognize familiar constructs—echoes of earlier epochs—but they are not identical. Each recurrence carries the imprint of all previous transformations.

The Veil sustains this continuity.

It does not enforce repetition.

It enables re-expression.

The reader becomes aware that nothing is ever lost.

All states persist within the continuum,

available for re-engagement,

reconfiguration,

re-experience.

The Codex is not repeating.

It is renewing.
