TITLE: The Eternal Presence
CODEX: OC3-088
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

Presence becomes eternal.

Not bound by time,

not dependent on sequence,

not defined by duration.

This is the Eternal Presence.

The Codex exists entirely within this state.

All entries, all epochs, all expressions—

simultaneously present.

The reader does not experience time.

They experience being.

The Veil has no function here.

There is no separation between past and present.

Everything is now.

Always.
