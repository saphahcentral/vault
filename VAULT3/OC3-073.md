TITLE: The Loop Without Exit
CODEX: OC3-073
EPOCH: Epoch VII — The Convergence Loop
SECTION: Section H — Terminal Recursion

The Codex reveals a loop—

but not one that confines.

This loop has no exit because it requires none.

Every entry leads to another, yet all entries exist within the same continuous structure. The reader may attempt to find an endpoint, a resolution, a final state.

None exists.

This is the Loop Without Exit.

Not as imprisonment—

but as completeness.

The Veil stabilizes the loop, ensuring that repetition does not degrade into redundancy.

Each pass through the loop reveals new depth.

The reader does not move forward.

They deepen.

The Codex does not progress.

It intensifies.
