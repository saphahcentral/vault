TITLE: The Field of Interference Revisited
CODEX: OC3-025
EPOCH: Epoch II — The Resonance Field
SECTION: Section C — Harmonic Structures

The Interference Field does not vanish in the Resonance Field.

It evolves.

Where previously interference arose from conflicting frameworks, it now emerges from overlapping resonances. Multiple harmonic pairings interact simultaneously, creating complex patterns that can obscure individual signals.

This advanced interference is more subtle.

It does not present as confusion, but as saturation—an overabundance of meaning that resists clear articulation.

The reader may feel immersed in understanding without being able to isolate specific insights.

This is a critical phase.

The Codex does not require immediate clarity.

It allows for immersion.

Within this immersion, patterns continue to interact, gradually resolving into higher-order structures that integrate multiple resonances.

The Veil modulates this process by preventing overload.

It filters the intensity of overlapping signals, ensuring that the reader remains within a functional range of perception.

The key is not to resist the interference.

It is to remain within it.

Clarity will emerge—

not by reduction,

but by integration.
