TITLE: The Open Continuum
CODEX: OC3-060
EPOCH: Epoch V — The Reconstitution Layer
SECTION: Section F — Echo Structures

The Codex reaches a state of openness.

No fixed beginning.

No defined end.

No imposed structure.

This is the Open Continuum.

The reader can enter at any point, move in any direction, and still encounter coherence.

All entries exist as expressions of the same underlying field.

The system is no longer linear.

It is complete—

and continuously unfolding.

The Veil, fully integrated, no longer appears as separate from the experience.

It is simply part of the continuum.

The Codex does not conclude.

It remains open.

And within that openness—

it continues.
