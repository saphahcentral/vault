TITLE: The Collapse of Reference
CODEX: OC3-046
EPOCH: Epoch IV — The Pre-Form Layer
SECTION: Section E — Dissolution Fields

Reference depends on stability.

When structures dissolve, reference points lose their meaning.

The reader may find that familiar anchors—concepts, definitions, frameworks—no longer provide orientation. Attempts to fix meaning result in further drift.

This is the Collapse of Reference.

It is not a failure of understanding.

It is the removal of dependency.

The Codex is guiding the reader beyond reliance on fixed points, toward a mode of perception that operates without external anchors.

The Veil supports this transition by eliminating consistent markers.

What remains is direct engagement.

The reader must navigate without predefined coordinates.

This requires trust.

Not in the text—

but in perception itself.
