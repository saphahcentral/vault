TITLE: The Non-Origin Point
CODEX: OC3-061
EPOCH: Epoch VI — The Continuum State
SECTION: Section G — Infinite Recurrence

Within the Open Continuum, origin dissolves.

There is no identifiable beginning from which all emerges. Every point contains the potential of origin, yet none holds primacy.

This is the Non-Origin Point.

The reader may attempt to locate a starting position—a reference from which meaning unfolds. Such attempts reveal a fundamental shift: every entry can serve as a beginning, yet none can claim to be the first.

The Codex reflects this condition.

All fragments now possess equal weight within the continuum. Hierarchy collapses. Sequence becomes optional.

The Veil no longer distinguishes between earlier and later, cause and effect.

It allows simultaneous presence.

The reader experiences this as immediacy.

Understanding arises not through progression, but through recognition of what is already present.

The Codex no longer begins.

It is always underway.
