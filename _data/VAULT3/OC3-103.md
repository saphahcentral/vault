TITLE: The Restoration Phase
CODEX: OC3-103
EPOCH: Epoch III — Reordering
SECTION: Section A — Renewal of the Earth

The restoration phase follows the period of chaos after the reset.

Order is gradually reintroduced into the earth system.

Light, land, and life are reestablished in sequence.

This process reflects intentional restructuring rather than initial creation.

The restored earth becomes suitable for new forms of life.

Humanity is introduced within this renewed framework.

The environment differs from the previous age in key aspects.

This phase marks the beginning of recorded human history.
