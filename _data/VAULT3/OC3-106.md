TITLE: Pre-Flood Conditions
CODEX: OC3-106
EPOCH: Epoch III — Pre-Flood
SECTION: Section D — Final State

The world before the flood reaches a peak of corruption.

Violence and disorder become dominant characteristics.

The natural order is disrupted across regions.

Human behavior reflects systemic breakdown.

The environment begins to respond to these conditions.

Judgment is determined as necessary.

A preservation plan is established.

This sets the stage for the flood event.
