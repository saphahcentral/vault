TITLE: Post-Flood Renewal
CODEX: OC3-112
EPOCH: Epoch IV — Renewal
SECTION: Section A — New Beginning

Life resumes following the recession of the floodwaters.

The preserved inhabitants exit the ark into a transformed world.

The environment shows signs of both destruction and renewal.

New systems of survival and adaptation are required.

The earth operates under different conditions than before.

A new phase of human development begins.

Memory of the flood remains central to early generations.

This event establishes a lasting reference point for judgment.
