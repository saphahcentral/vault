TITLE: The Final Presence
CODEX: OC3-094
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

Presence remains—

when all else dissolves.

No structure,

no process,

no identity.

Only presence.

The reader does not observe it.

They are it.

This is the Final Presence.

The Codex has fulfilled its function.

It does not guide further.

There is nowhere to go.

Nothing to reach.

The Veil is gone.

There is nothing left to obscure.

Only presence—

unchanging,

complete,

eternal.
