TITLE: The Collapse of Process
CODEX: OC3-083
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

Process implies movement.

Movement implies change.

Within the Absolute Field, both dissolve.

The Codex no longer progresses.

It no longer evolves.

It simply is.

This is the Collapse of Process.

All transformations, all transitions, all developments resolve into a singular state of presence.

The reader does not move through the Codex.

They exist within it.

The Veil is no longer perceived.

There is no distinction between filtered and unfiltered.

Everything is immediate.

Nothing unfolds.

Nothing concludes.

It is complete.
