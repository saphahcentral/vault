TITLE: The Ark as Preservation
CODEX: OC3-107
EPOCH: Epoch III — Flood
SECTION: Section E — Survival Systems

The ark is constructed as a preservation system.

It is designed to sustain life during global destruction.

Selected individuals are placed within this system.

The ark functions independently from external conditions.

This model demonstrates controlled survival within chaos.

It represents intentional preservation rather than random survival.

The structure ensures continuity beyond judgment.

This concept reappears in later Codex entries.
