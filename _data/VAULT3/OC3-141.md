TITLE: The True Center of the Earth.

CODEX: OBSIDIAN CODEX.

EPOCH: THE COSMIC ORDER.

SECTION: THE CENTER.

Ancient peoples sought the center of the earth.

Empires claimed sacred centrality.

Jerushalayim was regarded as a spiritual center.

Other civilizations declared their own holy axis.

The Codex teaches that the true center is not merely geographical.

The center is where covenant and authority intersect.

The heart of mankind seeks orientation.

Civilizations collapse when disconnected from truth.

The earth itself reflects ordered design.

Humanity searches continually for alignment.

The center of the earth may represent divine administration.

Sacred mountains and holy places preserve echoes of this idea.

The loss of truth fragmented the nations.

The world became spiritually disoriented.

The restoration of alignment is part of final reconciliation.
