TITLE: Throne Flora and Life Systems
CODEX: OC3-123
EPOCH: PREVIOUS AGE / CURRENT ERA
SECTION: Section L — Environmental Indicators

Certain regions contain unique plant life and ecosystems.

These systems differ from surrounding environments.

Flora may display medicinal or aromatic properties.

Such characteristics suggest specialized environmental conditions.

Changes in atmospheric composition may have influenced growth.

Larger and more diverse plant systems may have existed previously.

Current ecosystems may represent reduced versions of earlier forms.

These indicators support the concept of environmental variation across ages.
