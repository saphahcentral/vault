TITLE: Early Human Establishment
CODEX: OC3-104
EPOCH: Epoch III — Human Emergence
SECTION: Section B — First Societies

Humanity begins to establish order within the restored earth.

Communities form based on shared survival and purpose.

Knowledge is transmitted through direct experience and instruction.

The early generations retain awareness of prior events.

Spiritual and physical realities are closely linked.

Leadership structures begin to develop.

These systems lay the foundation for later civilizations.

The memory of the previous age gradually diminishes.
