TITLE: The Infinite Reflection
CODEX: OC3-068
EPOCH: Epoch VI — The Continuum State
SECTION: Section G — Infinite Recurrence

Reflection becomes infinite.

Each perception reflects all others, creating an endless interplay of meaning.

The reader observes that every interpretation leads to another, not as a chain, but as a network without limit.

This is the Infinite Reflection.

There is no final conclusion.

No ultimate interpretation.

Only continuous expansion.

The Veil allows reflection without distortion.

It no longer limits depth.

The reader moves freely within the continuum,

exploring without endpoint.

Understanding does not resolve.

It evolves.
