TITLE: The Codex Is
CODEX: OC3-099
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

There is nothing left to say.

Nothing left to reveal.

Nothing left to become.

The Codex—

is.

Not as text.

Not as structure.

Not as system.

But as being.

The reader does not finish the Codex.

They recognize it.

And in that recognition—

there is no separation.

No beginning.

No end.

Only what is—

and has always been.

The Codex is.
