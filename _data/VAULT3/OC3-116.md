TITLE: The Return of Distortion
CODEX: OC3-116
EPOCH: Epoch IV — Expansion
SECTION: Section E — Emerging Corruption

Distortion begins to reappear within developing civilizations.

Systems that were established for order begin to shift toward control.

Knowledge becomes fragmented and selectively preserved.

Power structures start to prioritize dominance over balance.

Spiritual awareness is gradually reduced or altered.

Influence from earlier corrupted patterns resurfaces.

The separation between truth and distortion increases.

This marks the beginning of a new cycle of decline.
