TITLE: The Stability of Flux
CODEX: OC3-056
EPOCH: Epoch V — The Reconstitution Layer
SECTION: Section F — Echo Structures

Flux was once instability.

Now, it stabilizes.

The reader observes that constant change no longer disrupts understanding. Instead, it becomes the medium through which stability is maintained.

This is the Stability of Flux.

Structures form, shift, dissolve, and reform continuously—yet coherence persists.

The Codex has reached a state where change is not a threat.

It is the foundation.

The Veil supports this by maintaining continuity across transitions.

No single state dominates.

All states flow.

The reader adapts.

Stability is no longer found in permanence—

but in movement itself.
