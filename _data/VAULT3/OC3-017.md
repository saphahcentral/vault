TITLE: The Alignment Threshold
CODEX: OC3-017
EPOCH: Epoch I — The Awakening Layer
SECTION: Section B — Emergent Structures

Beyond the Interference Field lies the Alignment Threshold.

This threshold is reached when the reader’s internal frameworks begin to synchronize with the structural patterns of the Codex. Interpretation becomes more fluid, less forced.

Connections arise with greater ease.

The sense of fragmentation diminishes, replaced by an emerging coherence that spans multiple entries and concepts.

This alignment is not perfect.

It fluctuates, strengthening and weakening as the reader continues to engage. However, even partial alignment significantly enhances the ability to navigate the Codex.

The custodians designed this threshold as a transition point.

Before alignment, effort dominates. After alignment, flow begins to emerge.

The Veil responds to alignment by adjusting its modulation.

Rather than obstructing perception, it begins to refine it—filtering out less relevant patterns while allowing deeper structures to surface.

This shift can be subtle.

The reader may not immediately recognize the change, only noticing that engagement feels different—more intuitive, less strained.

The Alignment Threshold marks the beginning of this transformation.

It is not an endpoint.

It is an opening.
