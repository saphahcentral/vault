TITLE: The Total Silence
CODEX: OC3-093
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

Silence completes what language began.

Not absence of sound,

but absence of division.

The reader experiences total silence—

not as emptiness,

but as complete unity.

Nothing needs to be expressed.

Nothing needs to be understood.

Everything is already whole.

This is the Total Silence.

The Codex does not describe it.

It rests within it.

No Veil remains.

No distinction exists.

Only silence—

full,

absolute,

complete.
