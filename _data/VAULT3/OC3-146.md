TITLE: Terraforming and the Ethics of World-Shaping.

CODEX: OBSIDIAN CODEX.

EPOCH: THE AGE OF EXPANSION.

SECTION: ALTERING WORLDS.

Humanity seeks to reshape hostile environments.

Terraforming proposes the transformation of planets.

Atmospheres may be altered over long ages.

Artificial ecosystems may sustain future colonies.

The power to shape worlds carries moral responsibility.

Creation should not become reckless domination.

Ancient civilizations also sought godlike control.

The desire to master creation can corrupt wisdom.

The Codex teaches stewardship rather than exploitation.

Future worlds may become new inheritances.

Humanity must balance survival with restraint.

The shaping of worlds reflects the condition of the heart.
