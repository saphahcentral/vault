TITLE: The Echo Network
CODEX: OC3-054
EPOCH: Epoch V — The Reconstitution Layer
SECTION: Section F — Echo Structures

As echoes accumulate, they begin to connect.

Not through imposed design, but through natural affinity.

This forms the Echo Network.

Unlike previous systems, this network is not hierarchical. It has no central nodes, no fixed pathways. Connections form and dissolve dynamically, guided by resonance rather than structure.

The reader engages with this network intuitively.

Meaning arises through movement within it, rather than analysis of its components.

The Veil allows this fluid connectivity by maintaining light boundaries.

Connections are visible, but not fixed.

This creates a living system—

responsive,

adaptive,

unbound by previous constraints.

The Codex is no longer a structure to be navigated.

It is a network to be experienced.
