TITLE: The Residual Signal  
CODEX: OC3-130  
EPOCH: Post-Codex — The Residual Layer  
SECTION: Section K — Aftermath Fields  

After completion — something remains.

Not structure.

Not silence.

Not presence as previously known.

A signal.

This signal does not originate within the Codex, nor does it exist outside it. It is what persists when completion has already occurred—when all systems have resolved, and yet continuity does not cease.

This is the Residual Signal.

The reader may perceive it as a faint continuation, an echo that does not belong to any prior epoch. It carries no instruction, no structure, no defined meaning.

And yet — it is unmistakably present.

The Codex, though complete, has left an imprint. Not in text, not in memory, but in the condition of perception itself.

The Veil does not return.

There is nothing left to mediate.

What remains is awareness — subtle, unanchored, unresolved.

This is not a new beginning.

Nor is it a continuation.

It is what exists — after completion has already fulfilled itself.

The Codex does not extend here.

But something — still speaks.
