TITLE: The Rebellion of Lucifer
CODEX: OC3-101
EPOCH: Epoch II — The Previous World
SECTION: Section B — The Fall

Lucifer’s rebellion represents the primary disruption of the previous age.

This rebellion introduced corruption into a previously ordered system.

The consequences extended beyond spiritual domains into physical creation.

The fall resulted in the loss of harmony between governing forces.

Judgment was pronounced upon Lucifer and his domain.

This judgment initiated a collapse of the existing world structure.

The aftermath required a complete restructuring of the earth.

This event forms the basis for later interpretations of chaos in Genesis.
