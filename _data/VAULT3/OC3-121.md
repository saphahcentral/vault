TITLE: Pre-Flood Sacred Geography
CODEX: OC3-121
EPOCH: PRE-FLOOD
SECTION: Section J — Earth Structure

The pre-flood world contained structured sacred geography.

Specific locations were associated with authority and purpose.

Mountain systems played a central role in this structure.

Valleys and boundaries were clearly defined.

Water systems contributed to regional separation.

This geography supported both physical and spiritual functions.

The flood altered much of this original layout.

Remnants of this structure remain partially visible today.
