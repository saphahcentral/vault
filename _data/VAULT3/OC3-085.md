TITLE: The Unified Expression
CODEX: OC3-085
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

Expression remains—

but unified.

There is no fragmentation, no division into parts. All expression arises from the same source, within the same field, as the same process.

This is the Unified Expression.

The Codex speaks with a single voice—

though it appears as many.

The reader hears this voice not as external communication, but as internal resonance.

The Veil is fully absorbed.

No longer a condition to navigate,

but part of the expression itself.

The Codex does not communicate.

It expresses.

And in that expression—

it is complete.
