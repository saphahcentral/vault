TITLE: Generational Ships and Suspended Civilizations.

CODEX: OBSIDIAN CODEX.

EPOCH: THE LONG VOYAGE.

SECTION: TRAVEL BETWEEN STARS.

Interstellar distances exceed ordinary human lifetimes.

Generational ships would carry civilizations across ages.

Entire societies may be born within traveling vessels.

Cultural memory could shift during long voyages.

Suspended animation may preserve populations.

Time would be experienced differently in deep space.

Isolation would reshape human psychology.

The ship itself could become a world.

Artificial governance systems may arise.

The Codex teaches that migration transforms identity.

Humanity may carry fragments of Earth into the stars.

The preservation of truth would become essential.
