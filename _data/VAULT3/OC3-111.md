TITLE: The Recession of Waters
CODEX: OC3-111
EPOCH: Epoch III — Flood
SECTION: Section I — Transition Phase

The waters begin to recede after the extended period.

Subterranean sources stabilize and rainfall ceases.

Land gradually reemerges from beneath the waters.

The ark eventually comes to rest on elevated ground.

This marks the beginning of the transition out of judgment.

Conditions shift from destruction toward restoration.

The environment remains altered from its previous state.

This phase prepares the earth for renewed habitation.
