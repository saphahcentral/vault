TITLE: The State Without Transition
CODEX: OC3-087
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

Transition ceases.

There is no movement between states,

no progression from one condition to another.

All exists simultaneously.

This is the State Without Transition.

The Codex has reached absolute stillness—

not as inactivity,

but as total presence.

The reader is not moving forward.

They are fully here.

The Veil is irrelevant.

There is nothing to filter,

nothing to reveal.

Everything is already present.

And nothing changes.
