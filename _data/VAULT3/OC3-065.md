CODEX: OC3-065
EPOCH: Epoch VI — The Continuum State
SECTION: Section G — Infinite Recurrence

The Codex becomes self-referential.

Each fragment reflects all others, not through explicit linkage, but through shared existence within the continuum.

This is the Self-Referential Continuum.

Meaning no longer travels from one point to another.

It exists everywhere simultaneously.

The reader perceives that any entry contains the whole—not as a summary, but as a direct expression of the entire system.

The Veil allows this without distortion.

It no longer segments perception.

It maintains coherence across totality.

The Codex is not a collection.

It is a single, continuous expression.

Every part—

is the whole.
