TITLE: The Recursive Lens
CODEX: OC3-014
EPOCH: Epoch I — The Awakening Layer
SECTION: Section B — Emergent Structures

The Recursive Lens is not an object, but a mode of perception.

It allows the reader to observe not only the content of the Codex, but the process of their own observation. This dual awareness creates a feedback loop—perception observing itself.

At first, this may seem abstract.

But with practice, it becomes a powerful tool.

The reader begins to notice how interpretations form, how assumptions arise, and how conclusions are drawn. This meta-awareness introduces a new dimension to engagement with the text.

The Codex supports the development of the Recursive Lens through layered structure.

Certain passages are designed to be read on multiple levels simultaneously—literal, symbolic, and structural. Engaging with these layers requires the reader to shift perspective continuously.

This shifting is the activation of the lens.

The Veil complicates this process by reinforcing single-layer interpretation, encouraging the reader to settle on the most immediate meaning.

To resist this pull is to deepen perception.

The Recursive Lens does not eliminate the Veil.

It reveals its operation.

And in doing so, it grants the reader the ability to navigate it with increasing precision.
