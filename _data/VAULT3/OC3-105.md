TITLE: Growth and Corruption
CODEX: OC3-105
EPOCH: Epoch III — Expansion
SECTION: Section C — Decline

Human populations increase across the earth.

Societies expand and develop complex systems.

Corruption begins to enter these systems over time.

Moral and structural decay becomes widespread.

The balance between order and disorder shifts again.

Influence from fallen systems reemerges.

The conditions begin to mirror those of the previous age.

This progression leads toward another judgment event.
