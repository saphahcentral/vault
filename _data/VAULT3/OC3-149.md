TITLE: The Psychology of Humans Born Under Alien Skies.

CODEX: OBSIDIAN CODEX.

EPOCH: THE AGE OF DISPERSION.

SECTION: THE MIND OF FUTURE HUMANITY.

Humans born away from Earth would inherit different perceptions.

The absence of Earth's sky would affect memory and identity.

Different day lengths would reshape sleep cycles.

Children raised on other worlds may not emotionally identify with Earth.

Myths and symbols would evolve differently.

Architecture would adapt to alien conditions.

Religion and philosophy may shift with environment.

Isolation from Earth could create cultural divergence.

The stars themselves would appear differently.

The Codex teaches that the heavens influence imagination.

Civilizations shaped by alien skies would think differently from their ancestors.
