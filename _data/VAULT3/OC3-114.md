TITLE: Expansion Across the Earth
CODEX: OC3-114
EPOCH: Epoch IV — Expansion
SECTION: Section C — Distribution

Populations begin to spread across the earth.

Different regions develop distinct characteristics.

Cultural and environmental variations increase.

Communication between groups becomes limited.

Diversity of systems emerges over time.

The memory of unity begins to fade.

Separate identities are formed.

This expansion shapes future civilizations.
