TITLE: The Rising Waters
CODEX: OC3-109
EPOCH: Epoch III — Flood
SECTION: Section G — Expansion Phase

The waters continue to rise beyond initial expectations.

All known elevations become submerged under increasing depths.

The earth transforms into a unified water system.

Movement becomes limited to what floats or survives within the waters.

The ark drifts without control across the surface.

This phase introduces prolonged uncertainty and isolation.

The duration extends far beyond the initial rainfall.

The expansion phase defines the severity of the event.
