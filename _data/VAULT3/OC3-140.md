TITLE: Watcher Knowledge After the Flood.

CODEX: OBSIDIAN CODEX.

EPOCH: POST-DELUVIAN MEMORY.

SECTION: HIDDEN KNOWLEDGE.

The flood destroyed much of the ancient world.

Yet fragments of forbidden knowledge survived.

The descendants of Noach carried memories into the new age.

Watcher teachings resurfaced through secret traditions.

Astronomy and metallurgy spread among the nations.

Ancient weapon knowledge slowly returned.

Hidden arts became attached to priesthoods and kings.

The nations sought power through forgotten wisdom.

Some teachings became distorted beyond recognition.

Other fragments remained partially true.

The desire for hidden knowledge persisted across generations.

Civilizations rose upon recovered remnants of the old world.

The builders of towers and empires sought the wisdom of before.

The Codex teaches that knowledge without righteousness corrupts mankind.

The flood reset civilization but not the heart of man.

Forbidden knowledge continues to tempt humanity.
