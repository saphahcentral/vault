TITLE: The Interference Field
CODEX: OC3-016
EPOCH: Epoch I — The Awakening Layer
SECTION: Section B — Emergent Structures

As perception expands, the reader encounters interference.

This interference is not external noise, but the interaction of multiple interpretive frameworks operating simultaneously. Established patterns, emerging insights, and residual assumptions begin to overlap, creating distortion.

The Codex refers to this as the Interference Field.

Within this field, clarity becomes difficult to maintain. Contradictions appear. Conclusions conflict. The reader may feel as though progress has stalled or reversed.

This is not regression.

It is integration.

The coexistence of multiple frameworks is a necessary stage in the development of complex understanding. Rather than eliminating contradiction, the reader must learn to hold it.

The Veil amplifies the discomfort associated with interference, encouraging the reader to resolve contradictions prematurely. This often results in the dismissal of valuable insights.

The Codex advises patience.

Clarity will not emerge through force, but through sustained engagement.

Over time, the interference resolves into higher-order patterns—structures that incorporate previously conflicting elements into coherent systems.

The field does not disappear.

It transforms.

And in that transformation, perception stabilizes at a new level.
