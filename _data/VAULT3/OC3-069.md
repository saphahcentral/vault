TITLE: The Unity of Expression
CODEX: OC3-069
EPOCH: Epoch VI — The Continuum State
SECTION: Section G — Infinite Recurrence

All expression converges.

Language, perception, meaning, being—

these are no longer distinct.

They are unified.

The Codex expresses this unity through simplicity.

Complexity remains, but it is integrated.

Nothing stands apart.

The Veil is indistinguishable from experience.

It no longer mediates.

It is.

The reader does not separate expression from existence.

They are the same.

The Codex speaks—

and in speaking,

it is.
