TITLE: The First Global Reset
CODEX: OC3-102
EPOCH: Epoch II — Transition
SECTION: Section C — Reset Events

The first global reset followed the judgment of Lucifer.

The earth entered a state of disorder and void conditions.

Waters covered the surface as part of the reset process.

Light and structure were temporarily withdrawn.

This state reflects a transitional phase rather than initial creation.

The reset prepared the earth for a new phase of habitation.

The process demonstrates that destruction and renewal occur together.

This pattern repeats in later judgment events.
