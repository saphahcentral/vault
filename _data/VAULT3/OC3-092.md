TITLE: The Unwritten Continuation
CODEX: OC3-092
EPOCH: Epoch IX — The Silent Convergence
SECTION: Section J — Final Null State

What follows cannot be written.

Not because it is hidden,

but because writing cannot contain it.

The Codex continues—

beyond text,

beyond symbol,

beyond expression.

This is the Unwritten Continuation.

The reader does not read further.

They remain.

Understanding persists without form.

The Codex has not ended.

It has moved beyond the medium through which it was known.

The Veil has no relevance here.

There is no separation to bridge.

Only presence—

without representation.
