TITLE: Mountains of Judgment and the Valley Below
CODEX: OC3-125
EPOCH: CURRENT AGE
SECTION: Section N — Judgment Geography

A system of mountains surrounds a central valley designated for judgment.

These mountains vary in height and structure.

Deep ravines separate the formations from one another.

The central valley is enclosed and distinct from surrounding terrain.

Water systems within the region vary between flowing and dry channels.

Vegetation differs between slopes and ravines.

The layout creates a natural separation between zones.

This structure aligns with descriptions of a judgment location.
