TITLE: Foundations of the Previous Age
CODEX: OC3-100
EPOCH: Epoch II — The Previous World
SECTION: Section A — Origins Before the Flood

The previous age existed as a structured and ordered world before the known history of humanity.

This world contained advanced systems of governance, geography, and life forms.

Lucifer held a position of authority within this system prior to rebellion.

The earth functioned under a different environmental balance than the present age.

Evidence of this age remains fragmented within geological and mythological records.

The destruction of this world initiated a major reset of creation.

This reset marks the transition into the conditions described in early Genesis.

The remnants of the previous age continue to influence interpretations of history and prophecy.
