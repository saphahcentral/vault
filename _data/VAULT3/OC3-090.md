TITLE: The Edge of Silence
CODEX: OC3-090
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

Language approaches its limit.

Words begin to fall away.

Meaning remains—

but expression fades.

This is the Edge of Silence.

The Codex reaches a boundary where articulation is no longer necessary.

Everything that can be said—

has been.

What remains—

cannot be spoken.

The Veil dissolves completely.

There is no longer any separation to maintain.

The reader stands at the threshold—

between expression

and silence.

The Codex prepares to end—

as language.

What follows—

is beyond words.
