TITLE: The Initiation of the Flood
CODEX: OC3-108
EPOCH: Epoch III — Flood
SECTION: Section F — Judgment Begins

The flood begins with the release of waters from both above and below.

Rain falls continuously as subterranean sources erupt across the earth.

The combined forces create a rapid rise in global water levels.

Structures and landscapes are quickly overwhelmed.

The separation between land and sea is temporarily removed.

This marks the formal initiation of judgment upon the world.

The ark remains the only stable environment during this phase.

The process confirms the scale of the global reset.
