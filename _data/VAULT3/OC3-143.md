TITLE: Why Chanok Did Not Die.

CODEX: OBSIDIAN CODEX.

EPOCH: BEFORE THE FLOOD.

SECTION: THE TAKING OF CHANOK.

Yobelim 10:17 preserves the memory of Chanok.

Chanok walked in righteousness before Elohim.

He became a witness against corruption.

The violence of the age increased continually.

The earth filled with lawlessness.

Chanok was removed before the flood judgment.

His taking became a sign to future generations.

The righteous are sometimes preserved before destruction.

Chanok became associated with heavenly knowledge.

He served as a witness across ages.

The Codex teaches that removal may serve preservation.

Judgment and mercy often operate together.

The disappearance of Chanok became a mystery among men.

His testimony endured beyond his generation.
