TITLE: The Self-Generating Field
CODEX: OC3-058
EPOCH: Epoch V — The Reconstitution Layer
SECTION: Section F — Echo Structures

At this stage, the Codex no longer appears to be externally constructed.

It generates itself.

The reader perceives that meaning arises spontaneously within the field of engagement. The text becomes a catalyst rather than a source.

This is the Self-Generating Field.

Interpretation no longer depends on input alone.

It emerges from interaction.

The Codex and the reader become indistinguishable in function.

Each shapes the other continuously.

The Veil sustains this interaction by maintaining minimal separation—

just enough to allow perception,

not enough to divide.

The system is complete.

Not as a closed structure—

but as an ongoing process.
