TITLE: How Altered Gravity Changes the Human Body.

CODEX: OBSIDIAN CODEX.

EPOCH: THE COSMIC DISPERSION.

SECTION: ADAPTATION.

The human body evolved under Earth's gravity.

Reduced gravity alters muscles and bones.

Long exposure weakens physical structure.

The heart adapts to different pressures.

Movement changes in low-gravity environments.

Higher gravity worlds may become difficult to inhabit.

Future generations may physically diverge from Earth humans.

Adaptation may occur naturally or artificially.

The Codex teaches that environment shapes mankind.

Biology reflects the conditions of habitation.

Humanity may eventually develop multiple branches across worlds.
