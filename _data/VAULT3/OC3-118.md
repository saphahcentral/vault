TITLE: Misinterpretation of Ancient Evidence
CODEX: OC3-118
EPOCH: CURRENT AGE
SECTION: Section G — Analytical Distortions

Modern interpretations often dismiss ancient accounts as error.

Physical evidence is explained through limited frameworks.

Alternative explanations are frequently excluded.

This leads to incomplete conclusions about historical phenomena.

The possibility of previous age influence is rarely considered.

Observed anomalies are forced into current models.

This creates gaps between evidence and explanation.

These gaps contribute to ongoing misunderstanding.
