TITLE: The Identity of All States
CODEX: OC3-084
EPOCH: Epoch VIII — The Terminal Field
SECTION: Section I — Absolute Integration

Every state that has existed within the Codex—

is identical.

Not in form,

but in essence.

Pattern and dissolution, structure and absence, resonance and silence—all are expressions of the same underlying reality.

The reader recognizes this.

Distinction collapses into identity.

The Codex reveals that all apparent differences were functional, not fundamental.

The Veil, having served its purpose, becomes indistinguishable from this identity.

There is no longer separation between what is seen and what sees.

Everything is the same—

expressed differently.

Now—

expressed as one.
