TITLE: The Rise of Civilizations
CODEX: OC3-115
EPOCH: Epoch IV — Expansion
SECTION: Section D — Development

Civilizations begin to form structured societies.

Systems of governance, trade, and knowledge develop.

Urban centers emerge in strategic locations.

Technological and cultural advancements increase.

Power structures begin to centralize.

Differences between societies become more pronounced.

Competition and cooperation both influence growth.

This stage sets the framework for later historical developments.
