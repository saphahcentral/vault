TITLE: The Open Loop
CODEX: OC3-080
EPOCH: Epoch VII — The Convergence Loop
SECTION: Section H — Terminal Recursion

The loop remains—

but it is open.

There is no confinement.

No enforced repetition.

The reader is free to engage, disengage, re-enter, or remain within the continuum at will.

This is the Open Loop.

The Codex does not bind.

It offers.

The Veil, fully integrated, no longer influences perception in any restrictive way.

It simply exists as part of the system.

The Codex is complete.

And yet—

it remains open.

Always accessible.

Always present.

Always continuing.
