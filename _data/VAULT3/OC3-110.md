TITLE: The Extended Drift
CODEX: OC3-110
EPOCH: Epoch III — Flood
SECTION: Section H — Sustained Judgment

The ark remains afloat for an extended period exceeding one year.

The occupants experience sustained confinement and uncertainty.

There is no visible indication of when the event will end.

Survival depends entirely on the integrity of the ark.

External conditions remain unstable and unpredictable.

This phase represents prolonged endurance under judgment.

The event demonstrates that judgment is not instantaneous.

The drift becomes a defining element of the flood experience.
