TITLE: Reestablishment of Order
CODEX: OC3-113
EPOCH: Epoch IV — Renewal
SECTION: Section B — Reorganization

Humanity begins reorganizing after the flood.

Communities form based on survival and shared experience.

New leadership structures emerge.

The environment dictates new patterns of living.

Knowledge from before the flood is partially retained.

Adaptation becomes essential for continued existence.

Order is gradually restored across regions.

This phase stabilizes the post-flood world.
